About Me

Learning designer with a passion for impact

I specialise in designing learner-centred experiences that bridge the gap between knowledge and performance. Whether it's a SCORM module for compliance training or a Canvas course for higher education, I focus on what the learner needs — not just what looks good.

I work across the full instructional design lifecycle: from needs analysis and learning objective writing, through storyboarding and development, to evaluation and iteration.

Design Philosophy

  • Learner-centred design before aesthetics
  • Clear, measurable learning objectives first
  • Active recall over passive consumption
  • Accessibility built in from the start
  • Evaluate, iterate, and improve continuously
  • Context-appropriate technology choices

My Process

From analysis to evaluation

I follow the ADDIE framework adapted with agile touchpoints for faster iteration and continuous improvement.

A

Analyse

Needs analysis, audience profile, gap identification, and performance goals.

D

Design

Learning objectives, content outline, storyboard, and assessment design.

D

Develop

Build prototypes, develop assets, create interactions and produce final content.

I

Implement

Deploy to LMS, configure tracking, onboard learners and facilitators.

E

Evaluate

Kirkpatrick Levels 1–4: reaction, learning, behaviour, and results.
